// 03 — Projects

Selected Work

01 / B.E. CAPSTONE RESEARCH
SUSTAINABLE PAVER BLOCK RESEARCH
Problem: Construction waste disposal costs cities thousands per tonne — and conventional paver blocks ignore this resource entirely.

Designed and tested paver block mixes incorporating plastic waste (PP/HDPE) and demolition aggregate at 5%, 10%, 15%, and 20% replacement levels. Conducted compressive strength tests at 7, 14, and 28-day curing intervals to identify the optimal mix. Prepared a full cost-benefit analysis and material quantity calculations in MS Excel showing economic and environmental advantages.

Material TestingMix Design Strength AnalysisMS Excel Sustainability
15% plastic waste replacement identified as optimum mix — achieving target compressive strength while significantly reducing cost and diverting waste from landfills. Aligned with Swachh Bharat Mission objectives.
02 / INTERNSHIP DELIVERABLE · CIDCO
PMAY SITE LAYOUT & ROAD CORRIDOR
Problem: The CIDCO PMAY Package-3 site lacked a consolidated digital drawing set integrating all infrastructure elements at a single working scale.

Recreated the complete site layout in AutoCAD 2018 — road network, EWS/LIG building footprints, drainage alignment, and open spaces — plotted at 1:500 scale. Built the road corridor, ground surface model, and drainage pipe networks in Civil 3D. Prepared a comprehensive BOQ in MS Excel with VLOOKUP-linked rate libraries and PivotTable cost dashboards. Developed a construction schedule in MS Project and identified the critical path.

AutoCAD 2018Civil 3D MS Excel BOQMS Project Quantity Take-off
Delivered a full design package — site layout, cut/fill quantities, cost estimate, and Gantt schedule — presented to the project team under Shapoorji Pallonji SPE&C at Navi Mumbai.
